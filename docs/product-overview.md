# Conflict Resolution - Product Overview

## Purpose
Conflict Resolution is a case-based AI coaching tool for people navigating tense personal, work, or legal disputes. It helps users analyze incoming messages and draft responses that de-escalate, hold boundaries, or apply pressure.

## Core goals
- Reduce emotional escalation by turning raw messages into structured analysis.
- Provide clear, tactically distinct reply options for different outcomes.
- Create an auditable, exportable case history for personal or professional records.
- Keep the experience fast for common disputes and deep for high-stakes conflicts.

## Primary features
- Case setup with conflict context first, package selection below the description, and adversary confirmation in Mission Profile.
- War Room analysis with summary, detected tactics, legal risk gauge, and response modes.
- Response modes: Peacekeeper, Barrister, Grey Rock, Nuclear.
- Session-based plans: Standard and Premium mediation sessions (1 session = 1 analyzed round).
- Session ledger with monthly rollups and daily drilldowns.
- Demo scenarios to experience Standard and Premium flows without sessions.
- Template library for common conflict scenarios.
- Case vault with export to Markdown and print/PDF.
- Testimonials and Help Center for support and guidance.

## Intended audience
- Individuals handling relationship, family, landlord/tenant, workplace, or customer disputes.
- Professionals who need concise, defensible messaging and documentation.

## Product language and tone
Strategic, calm, and practical. The UI leans toward a "war room" aesthetic while keeping guidance clear and grounded.
